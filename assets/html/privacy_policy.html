Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 September 2024

Kristina Martirosyan ("we", "our", or "us") operates the VerifEye mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and photo (optional).
  • Link Data: URLs entered for safety checks.
  • Image Data: Images uploaded by users and associated metadata (such as capture date and time).

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • Registration: When you create an account.
  • Link Submissions: When you enter a URL to check its safety.
  • Image Uploads: When you upload an image to get metadata and analysis.
  • App Usage: Automatically when you use the App, including interaction data and usage patterns.

3. Data from Children

Our App is intended for general use and not specifically directed at children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Link Safety: Evaluate the safety of URLs you enter.
  • Image Metadata: Provide information about the capture date and time of uploaded images.
  • Improve Our Services: Enhance app functionality and user experience based on usage data.

Your data is not shared with any third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store your data in secure, encrypted, word-class databases. User data is retained until the user requests deletion of their account and associated data.

6. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Deletion: You can view, delete your account, and request the removal of your data from the application settings.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to you via in-app notifications or email.

8. Legal Compliance

We comply with relevant data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@verifeye.com.
